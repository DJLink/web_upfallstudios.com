Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy was last updated on November 4th 2023.

Upfall Studios has the following games:

  • Quest of Dungeons

and the following websites:

  • upfallstudios.com
  • questofdungeons.com

This page informs you of our data privacy policies.

This privacy policy applies to all our websites and games listed above. Our goal is to collect as little as possible about our customers while still effectively selling our games.

Website Tracking

We do not perform any user tracking on our websites.

On our Steam pages for our games we enable Google Analytics anonymized stats to help us better understand from where our potential players are hearing about our game. We use this information improve how we present our game to the public. Google Analytics tracking can be disabled here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Quest of Dungeons

The Steam version collects anonymized statistics for each play session for the statistic provided publicly on this website. We do no collect any personally identifiable information.

Mailing List

We operate a mailing list through MailChimp. When you sign up, we store (on Mailchimp's servers) your email address and we use this solely to contact you about our updates on our games and company business. We never have and never will share this data with any other 3rd parties. MailChimp also commits to securely storing your data safely. Their privacy policy and tools are available here: https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/

Game Engine

We use a custom engine for our games.

YouTube Embeds

Our game sites uses video embeds from youtube. We have activated "Restricted Data Tracking", so no tracking is done unless the user explicitely plays the video. The video embeds are required for correct operation of our website. Youtube's data privacy policy can be found here: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en&gl=en

Humble Bundle Widget

We use the Humble Bundle widget to sell the game directly to customers on our website. This widget may use cookies for processing purchases. The widget embed is required for correct operation of our website and business. Humble Bundle's data privacy policy can be found here: https://www.humblebundle.com/privacy

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Site. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
